So you want to be a developer?

Becoming a developer is an extremely rewarding thing. You gain an immense amount of technical skill, but none of this skill should overshadow the real intellectual development: you are learning how to learn.

Work anywhere. Work on anything.

A developer carries all of their skills in their head and their hands. The laptop is the medium through which the head and the hands create something new. A developer can work anywhere, and a developer can work in any industry. Technology is everywhere.

Never stop Learning

Before you decide to become a developer, understand the contract you are making with the universe: you must never stop learning. With this responsibility, comes unlimited possibility.
